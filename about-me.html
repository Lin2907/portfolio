I didn’t begin in tech. My first career was in the airline industry, navigating schedules and systems. But I’d always been drawn to creating things that lived on the internet — especially the kind that moved, pulsed, and came alive through animation.

Curious about what happens behind the scenes, I expanded my skills into full-stack development, complementing my background in web design. Since 2020, I’ve been working in e-commerce, combining creativity and technical know-how to build smooth, engaging user experiences.

When I’m not coding or designing, you can find me hanging out with friends, or experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen — all fueled by way too much coffee!
